Remedial Sketches - Drawings From My Sketchbooks



Lewis Rossignol's newest book; a collection of the most compelling pages from his sketchbooks.

5,00,000



Remedial Sketches is a collection of my favorite pages from my sketchbooks,
many of which I've never shown before. I have compiled them in a way that shows the variety of styles and methods that I use when exploring each subject.
To me, sketchbooks are the most intimate of all art forms because the drawings inside are not usually made to be seen by other people.
Through this engaging book you get to take an intimate tour through my sketchbooks, and mind.


About Me

    I am a freelance illustrator from Portland, Maine who enjoys working with mixed mediums.
    I am constantly building a portfolio of portraits, editorial work, and personal work.
    The personal work includes my sketchbooks, which are my absolute favorite thing to work on.

    The reason I love drawing in my sketchbooks is because it is the most therapeutic,
    or remedial, thing that I do in my studio practice; hence the name of the book.
    I have had tics caused by Tourette Syndrome since I was young.
    The tics are cyclical, and can get really severe and debilitating, especially when I feel anxious.
    To me, anxiety feels like there is all kinds of pressure built up inside of me,
    and the tics are my body way of releasing the pressure.
    I have discovered that while I am drawing, my tics subside.
    It is as if the pressure is being released through my pen instead of my face,
    and it is such a relief.
    For this reason I am constantly sketching,
    and have filled over 1,000 sketchbook pages in the last 12 months alone.

    Remedial Sketches is a book that is very personal because of how important sketching has become to me.
    I have spent the past few months compiling and organizing work from my sketchbooks to find my favorite pages.
    Because all of the drawings have meaning to me for one reason or another,
    it has been hard narrowing them down to what will be included in the book.
    I currently have a few different versions of the book ranging from 144 - 192 pages,
    and ultimately one of them will be the final product.
    This means that the book will be at least 144 pages, but possibly more depending on the success of the campaign.
    Because the book will be 100% complete while the campaign is still running,
    it will be sent to the printers the second this campaign wraps up,
    and the printer has agreed to print and rush-ship books for those who pre-order through this campaign.
    This means that by supporting the campaign you are guaranteeing that you will receive
    your books up to 8 weeks sooner than anyone else.
    By contributing to the campaign you are also helping me to publish this meaningful book,
    and I am very grateful to you for helping me make this a reality.
