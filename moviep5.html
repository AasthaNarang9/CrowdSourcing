Science With Sophie: Science Comedy for Everyone



Bigger, Smarter, Braver, Sillier, and Science-ier than ever.

5,00,000



Science With Sophie is a science comedy series for girls and everyone. Created by science educator and comedian Sophie Shrand, she and the cast of wacky characters - all played by Sophie - invite viewers to be brave, curious, funny, smart scientists every day. By showcasing female role models to people of all ages and gender identities, SWS is increasing gender equity through STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math). SWS needs your help to reach millions of young learners in Season 2!





Why this show? Why now?

    Science With Sophie uses comedy to close the gender gap in STEM. Women make up half of the worlds population, yet only 30% of US STEM careers are held by women, with only 12% held by women of color. Why does it happen? A major driving force in a childs self-identity is presence - or lack - of role models. It is a simple, scary idea: if you cannot see it, you cannot be it.
    Science With Sophie is a free, interactive series that engages kids, parents, teachers and anyone who loves life, bringing all ages and backgrounds together to spark conversation, scientific debate, and joyful inquiry about the world around us. By connecting viewers worldwide with female STEM role models, we can create change that lasts for decades as we inspire the next generation of young scientists. The show is just the beginning.
