HARGRAVE





In the darkness you will find, that which your soul already knows.

5,00,000



We want to be able to bring HARGRAVE to life, as close as possible to the original idea.
By crowdfunding this production, we are able to make the show ourselves and retain creative and financial control.





Detective, MATTHEW HARGRAVE, has had enough of department politics, cold cases and manipulation of the case results for the benefit of third parties. This is not the career he envisioned. In the midst of serious doubts about his career and life, a mysterious woman (EVELYN MEAD) contacts Hargrave about her missing husband. After a series of life altering events involving his partner (JULES KINCAID), and his informant (ROGER MURPHY), Hargrave knows he must make a change, again, but with that being all he knows in life is caught in limbo. Until a new case brings him and another Detective team (MASON CROWLEY and LOGAN CARMICHAEL) together to find the answers.




Written, Produced and Directed by Tanya M. Razo
Tanya M. Razo has worked in the entertainment industry since age 16. An active member of the Producers Guild of America (PGA) for over 17 years, she developed most of her career in the production world, at 20th Century Fox, in Los Angeles, CA. From exciting live broadcasts, to diligent nightly news and cutting edge post production, Tanya has hands on experience with a broad range of production workflows and media.
Raised in Los Angeles, born in Mexico, from Spanish-Mexican descent, her sensibilities run along a more European mind-set. Always keeping in touch with her passion for storytelling through the medium of photography which then led to writing. She has loved and studied film most of her life, and always wanted to put what she has learned to use, telling one of the many stories she had in her. Tanya believes that through the art of storytelling we can still remind ourselves that we already have all we need to survive, and flourish as human beings.






Making a movie or tv show costs money. Each part of the process requires a significant investment.
