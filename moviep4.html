Exotic Blaque



She is just like you, but black!

5,00,000



A proof of concept for a series that explores the complexity and nuance of blackness through the lens of a young,
black girl-next-door type growing up in a utopian world full of promise, progress, equal opportunity...
and slavery reparations! The series is propelled through the lens of science-fiction with underlying themes such as technology,
advancement, and the negative impacts of having too much of a good thing.





The Team


Lauryn Henry (Writer, Director, Actress)




    Lauryn has always held a deep appreciation for art in all of its forms. She has spent years of her youth training as both a dancer and a vocalist through musical theater, in addition to having taught herself in both the fields of photography and filmmaking.
    Lauryn is now a junior at the University of Southern California studying Cinema & Media Studies as well as pursuing a double minor in Entertainment Industry and Screenwriting. She is a YoungArts alumna, having been selected as a 2016 national winner for her accomplishments in the Cinematic Arts.
    ​ In recent years, in addition to writing and directing numerous shorts, she has taken on freelance work as a production designer, sound designer, and editor. Her work has been previously released with GirlBoss and PAPER Magazine.


Chandler Guzman,Producer




    Chandler Yvanna, also known under her producing title 'cult kid', is a sophomore at the University of Southern California as a Media Arts + Practice student in the School of Cinematic Arts. She specializes in ethical media making and focuses those efforts on producing films she believes rewrite the narrative of ethics in the entertainment industry. As a Los Angeles native and Chicana, she loves diving into projects that are based in the experiences of people of color.
    In a similar vein, Chandler also authors her media to re-appropriate the depiction of addiction, recovery, and post-generational trauma. As a film mentor for sober youth in lower-income communities Chandler's ultimate goal is to provide access to the world of cinema and story telling that she feels privileged to be apart of.


Brandon Le, Cinematographer




    Brandon Le is a recent graduate from USC’s Marshall School of business, where he simultaneously engaged in advanced business curriculum during the week and used any free time to explore cinematography and enhance his skills. He was born in Portland, Oregon, but has also lived a third of his life overseas in Taipei, Taiwan and Shanghai, China. Despite never having one single place to call home, he found stability in pursuing his passion for art through the performing arts and photography and cinematography. As a cinematographer, Brandon specializes in the visual aspects of filmmaking. He is most fascinated by sculpting light to craft artistic images, rather than merely capturing them. This has led him to work with many directors to create stunning music videos, short films, commercials, and documentaries. He has also worked with acclaimed filmmaker, Freddie Wong, at RocketJump, and is now working to expand his knowledge of lighting by working at Aputure, an LED lighting manufacturer.


Ways you can help

We will be shooting over the course of three weekends July 2019, and a significant percentage of the budget is allocated towards camera rentals and various equipment. Additionally, the world of this project is highly stylized, on top of having many science-fiction visual elements and sequences. With this is mind, art department plays a huge factor in the total budget as it includes costumes, hair, makeup, and props. Other costs include craft services (food) and securing locations.
We understand that $15,000 is a large number, but considering the demands of this project, it's well worth it and your money surely will not go to waste.
We appreciate anything you can do to help, so regardless of whether or not you choose to donate, please share this page and spread the word about this project! Feel free to share on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or whatever else you can think of! We just want to prove that this story works, that the world is interesting, and that the characters of Exotic Blaque are innovative and worth watching, so let's get people interested!
Thank you for your support!
from Lauryn & the Team
