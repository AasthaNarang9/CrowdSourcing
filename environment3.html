Nepal's 1st Elephant Refuge, Be Part of History!




Support Elephant Welfare and Community Evolution! Travel to Nepal, See Your Contribution 1st Hand!



13,50,000



Overview

Nepal’s First Elephant Refuge for rescued and retired Elephants is ready to happen with your help and we have great incentives for you when you make the decision to be part of history! The tourism in Nepal is evolving.
elephant rides are phasing out and the animals and local people are in desperate need of food, care, fencing, land and jobs. Please join us as we embark on this amazing journey. You'll be rewarded with trips to our refuge to see how you contributed first hand!.


Project Summary


We are ready to build the first refuge; our elephants are waiting, we have the property and we have a great team. Now we need you to help us make the first place in Nepal that elephants will run free during the day and rest in their corrals at night; swim in the river, be with their babies and learn to be elephants again.

The Impact

We will hire Local! Nepal’s youth suffers from unemployment; we need 15 to 20 people year one to come on board with us. By year 3 we should be hiring 50 people. As we grow the tourist industry in the area which has collapsed will start to recover.
Our jobs will have a just wage; health insurance and above all respect and dignity. We will provide two scholarships each year to local youths who wish to pursue a career in Animal Care at our local college.
We will save the precious water here. We will have a river warden to test and analyze the water of the river not only in our area but the entire river; monitoring usage and helping to change any behavior that is harmful to the water source. We will become the center of training for mahouts; elephant caregivers in Nepal. Here we will teach humane training. We will continue to have foot care clinics.
More elephants; we will continue to evaluate the 108 private elephants in Nepal and our board will determine at least once per year which elephants should be retired from the community and then we will negotiate their retirement with the private owner with the stipulation that the elephant cannot be replaced. We already have a list of 6 elephants that desperately need our help. Desperate is not an exaggerated word here.

The Perks and Rewards

We have several vacation packages suited for all levels! We hope you find one that works for you! Please contact us with any questions, we are able to make adjustments if needed. Trips need to be completed by 18 months from the day the campaign is completed. If you're not up for travel we also have options for donations. You may also qualify for tax deductions. Please note that our trips start in Nepal and do not include any International travel that may be required. Please make sure you are eligible for travel to Nepal prior to selecting a package.
Trip Costs include: All transportation within Nepal, Lodging including breakfast. Local guide service. All listed activities.

8 Day Tour of Nepal for 2 People. All the highlights of Nepal! Everest, Sunsets, Jungle! Day 1: Start your trip in Kathmandu where your guide will greet you at the airport and bring you to your hotel in the heart of the city. Get settled tour the local area, dine and shop.

Day 2: Everest! Wake early for the flight of a lifetime, Everest Experience. After the flight return to the hotel for lunch and an afternoon of tour the world heritage sites of the city.

Day 3: Travel Day! Fly to Chitwan National Park where you will stay at Sapana Village Lodge and see our Elephants in person. Spend the day with the elephants learn about care, feeding, bathing and playing. Have dinner with our Elephant experts and learn about our plans for the future and how you’ve helped these majestic animals.

Day 4: Jungle day! Morning canoe trip down the river where you will see amazing wildlife and many crocodiles! Down the river be picked up by a jeep safari which will take you deep into the jungle to see Rhinoceros, Tigers, Bears, Deer and serene scenery.

Day 5: Rest and Relax Day! Catch up on some needed R&R at the Spa, participate in our Meditation and Yoga program. Meet the women of our Skill Center and have some items made to commemorate your trip. Visit the Tharu village where you will have dinner and dance with the local people, experiencing the culture.

Day 6: Travel by private car towards Kathmandu. Spend the night at the Daman Hotel with panoramic views of Everest!

Day 7: Continue the journey to Kathmandu to prepare for your journey home. Shop the town, pick up last-minute gifts for friends and family! Stay overnight in the heart of the city!

Day 8: See you soon! Private car to the airport.
