Welcome to Ficterra.
We are part of a community of gamers that want a better, moddable, multi-player crafting experience.
We think we've built that with Ficterra. We need your help to finish our vision and launch it to the world.
This campaign will fund those finishing touches.

About Ficterra

    Ficterra is an effort to elevate crafting games to a new plateau. It is a 3D Sandbox Crafting Adventure with in-game modding that you can explore solo or with friends. Countless hours have made the game ready for Beta testing - now we need your help to polish and finish Ficterra.




Game overview

    Ficterra starts you with minimal resources in a world filled with diverse biomes and possibilities. Acquire resources to build a limitless inventory of materials to make tools, buildings, and gadgets. Avoid monsters and other hazards while exploring and expanding your world. Each biome offers a variation of landscape and available materials, as well as dangers. Continue to make more sophisticated tools and gadgets. Share with other Ficterra players.


Why you make a difference

    As good as the game currently is in Alpha state, your support and input will take it over the top. If you are passionate about testing, you will make a difference. Alternate perspectives and gameplay methods expose issues and drive further innovation. Bring a friend and test multi-player for us! Play Ficterra. Enjoy Ficterra. Talk Ficterra. Social media interaction attracts more players, so share with friends. More players expands our ability to continue to enhance the game experience based on your feedback playing the game.
