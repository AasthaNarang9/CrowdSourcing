Build Prosperity - A New Take on the Classic Citybuilder

1,209,259



Build Prosperity with New Cities, A fresh take on take on the classic game.

About the game

    Build your city - From a small farm town to a thriving metropolis.
    You are the Mayor, and you lead your city with impunity. There are no councilmembers here every decision, every victory and setback, it all comes down to you. Build your city from the ground up, rural town to metropolitan jewel, calling all the shots along the way.
    Learn their names - Track individual people and businesses.
    Peek into the lives of your citizens and follow them through their daily routines. See where they work, play, and sleep. Big Brother Mayor is watching. And hopefully, you are benevolent.
    Plan for traffic - Upgrade roads and plan highway networks.
    Carefully place your citys roads to create the circulatory system that keeps it humming. Make sure to leave room for upgrades once your citizens outgrow the humble two-lane road
    Parks and schools - Create a livable city -- to collect more taxes.
    Add all the amenities to keep your citizens happy. Word of mouth gets around, and you will find your population booming in short order. More citizens mean more money, and often more problems in turn.




A word from Lone Pine:

    I have developed and played games since childhood. I think that the time is right for a game that focuses on creative approaches to city living. Players can design residential areas with parks, museums, schools and retail centers. They can build industrial centers, office complexes and farms. All of this is connected by roads, and highways. Over the past year, I have developed a city builder with a traffic simulation and customizable 3D rendered buildings. In the past few months I have fully implemented The Prosperity System which will define the game: its a dynamic economic model where neighborhoods thrive or fail based on access to work, shopping, parks, and schools.


Why you make a difference
