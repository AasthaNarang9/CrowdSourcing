CANDLES BY TRAVIS YORK



Help get my candle company, lit!

5,00,000



With the help of love & theft, I finally learned that you have to take risks to get anything you want in this life.





By accident, I fell in love with making candles - & a host of people fell in love with the ones I made.

A Most Accidental Beginning

    In July of 2017, I stumbled into the world of candle making because of an article I read at the barbershop about a local company. All I was interested in was making something special for my Mom's birthday, as well as for my special someone, so I inquired. Within a month, I was apprenticing for the founder of said candle company, and shortly after, was encouraged to come up with my own line. A holiday candle I created all on my own had an initial run of 100, which sold out within two weeks. Sadly, it was too good to be true. In early 2018, it became apparent I wasn't getting paid what I was owed or receiving proper credit for what I'd created. Worst of all, I had convinced friends and family to buy from this company, and now I felt like a chump. Cheated, stolen from, and humiliated, I took my wax and went home. Until I realized: FORGET THAT MESS. Accidentally, I had found a unique skill that could become a going concern. Something to make and be proud of; and a way to be creative and show people the world as I feel it through our most powerful sense - smell. I have one sensitive schnoz. I have a passion for music & literature. And I have one big crazy, sentimental, geeky heart. By combining all these and putting aside the fear I've always had in asking for what I want, I'm beyond excited to welcome you to the world of STRANGE GENT CANDLE CO. I'm asking for $3,000 to help me get this baby up and running. I feel confident in saying that those of you reading this who have gotten the candles I've made have been big fans of my work. (And not just because they were free!) So now I'm asking, can I get your help? Raising this money will help ease my financial burden and keep me from accruing more debt.


What I Need To Make The Donuts (Candles)

1. Initial Batch of High Grade Organic Oils (the smelly stuff!): ~$800
2. Initial Batch of High Grade Soy Wax (approximately 200 lbs): ~$200
3. Initial Candle-Making Hardware (pitchers, burners, wicks, extension cords, etc.): ~$450
4. Labels, stickers, boxes, vessels, final design logo: ~$300
5. Website domain purchase,contract, and online store set up: $150
6. Stamps.Com Set up and account: TBD
7. Rental of work space: ~$150/per month
And so on. I'm asking for a little over what I need initially so I can have some reserve,
as supplies go quickly. I have to spend money to make money, and as many of you know,
that's not always easy. So in my asking you for help,
I've got some really amazing perks to hopefullybentice you to help this strange gentleman out.
Any and every little bit will help.


Even if you can't help monetarily, you can support by spreading the word to others who like stuff that smells good. Real good. I'm talking real, real, REAL good.
