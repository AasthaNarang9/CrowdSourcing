COFFEE TO TACKLE CLIMATE CHANGE: We provide clean energy, education & support for local farmers. 30% of Indonesians are farmers & do not have access to clean energy! They usually get only 3% of coffee's total retail price. We promise 30%: a fair coffee price, biogas installation & climate field school.

1) YOU take part in our crowdfunding campaign,
2) WE provide farmers with clean biogas energy & an education in climate-smart agriculture,
3) ALL of us enjoy safer climate!





The sooner you back this project, the better the energy can be saved.


Goals

We are Lil Lotus CoFFee team: Lyla, Ryoma, Campbell, Jazz & Andreas. We follow the adolescent program at Montessori School Bali. We represent the next generation , and as such, learning about sustainability & how we can improve the planet will be key for the future.
Business is part of our curriculum. We partnered up with su-re.co & their sustainable coffee, helping local farmers with clean energy & climate education.
The skills we have gained from this whole experience include organisation, marketing, communication, time management, research, science, mathematics & problem solving. Our learning is academic & authentic in nature. We would like to thank su-re.co team so much for their help & support. Enabling us to visit directly farmers was an experience we will never forget.





In order to keep their important work going, please donate to su-re.co crowdfunding campaign. Money that is raised will go directly into educating farmers. Lil Lotus CoFFee would like to donate $35 USD to the campaign.





Sustainable coffee: Selling coffee beans bought at a fair price directly from farmers to raise funds. Climate field school: Hosting farmers through the school at every village we buy coffee beans from, to teach climate-smart agriculture. Biogas and organic agriculture: Installing biogas digesters to produce clean renewable energy and organic fertilisers for farmers.



2 million smallholders coffee farmers living in remote villages across the country. 1 million biogas digesters are needed in Indonesia, estimated by biogas experts, BIRU. A conventional fixed biogas digester costs approximately $1000 USD including installation and training. Our easily transportable digester costs $300 USD including installation and training without subsidy.
